Classic Waffles

Classic waffles are a delicious breakfast anywhere in the world.

You are able to eat this amazing meal with multiple different additions such as some fruits if you like the sweet side, or some meat if you prefer the savory side.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 4 teaspoons of baking powder
  • 2 tablespoons of white sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 and a half cups of warm milk
  • one third of cup butter,melted
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, mix together flour, salt, baking powder and sugar; set aside. Preheat waffle iron to desired temperature.
  2. In a separate bowl, beat the eggs. Stir in the milk, butter and vanilla. Pour the milk mixture into the flour mixture; beat until blended.
  3. Ladle the batter into a preheated waffle iron. Cook the waffles until golden and crisp. Serve immediately.

Nutrition Facts

Per Serving: 379 calories; protein 10.2g; carbohydrates 47.6g; fat 16.2g; cholesterol 112.8mg; sodium 899.1mg.
